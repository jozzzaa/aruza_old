| Growth Hacking

A savvy restaurant hack instantly gained us

50% MORE FROM EACH CUSTOMER

A tiny burger location that already had a great product, but only two tables to dine in. We were immediately facing strong limitations. One of the various tests we applied was to put up new signage and redo the blackboards in the store to promote add-on products. After hours of watching the sale process at the POS system we discovered 80% of customers did not look up but grabbed an old A4 menu on the bench to order.

We created a large bench size menu that took customers through the order process and helped them build meals. This one tactic promptly increase the average customer spend by 50%.

THE CAMPAIGN THAT TOOK A DYING BUSINESS &

doubled the turnover in a month

A bakery chain with stores positioned in poor locations was continually suffering from lack of customers, and revenue was strongly subjective to seasonality. As a short term solution we crafted a free cake giveaway to every customer that came to the store. This was marketed through print and digital advertising. Each cake cost 30 cents to get a customer in doors and 70% of customer bought additional items which more than covered the cost.

This campaign attracted over 1,000 new customers to our stores and was a catalyst for strong business growth.
